About Us

Neighbourhood Cafe & Bar is a warm, welcoming spot nestled in La Lucia, Durban's north coast. We believe in the power of great food, quality coffee, and genuine community, because loving your neighbour starts with sharing a table.

From our hearty Full English to our signature Crayfish Mac 'n Cheese, every dish is made fresh with locally sourced ingredients. Whether you're here for a morning latte, a leisurely brunch, or an evening out, you're always welcome.
